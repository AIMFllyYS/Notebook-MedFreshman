期末复习工作站是一款革命性的多学科AI学习助手，通过深度分析课堂录音逐字稿，为学生打造沉浸式备考体验。核心亮点包括：基于真实课堂内容的详尽原创笔记，完美数学公式渲染；上下文感知的AI智能对话，支持划词提问获得精准解答；Python Manim驱动的数学动画，将抽象概念可视化；可交互组件让学生动手探索，深度理解知识内在逻辑。目前支持概率论与数理统计、有机化学、中国近现代史纲要、毛泽东思想和中国特色社会主义理论体系概论等多学科，采用可扩展架构便于接入新学科。技术栈基于Next.js 16、TypeScript、Tailwind v4，结合Zustand状态管理和KaTeX公式渲染，提供现代化、高性能的学习平台。

Final Exam Review Workstation is a revolutionary multi-disciplinary AI learning assistant that creates an immersive exam preparation experience through deep analysis of classroom recording transcripts. Core highlights include: detailed original notes based on real classroom content with perfect mathematical formula rendering; context-aware AI intelligent dialogue supporting text-selection queries for precise answers; Python Manim-driven mathematical animations that visualize abstract concepts; interactive components allowing students to explore and deeply understand the internal logic of knowledge. Currently supports Probability and Mathematical Statistics, Organic Chemistry, Modern Chinese History, and Mao Zedong Thought, with an extensible architecture for easy integration of new subjects. Built on Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind v4, combined with Zustand state management and KaTeX formula rendering, providing a modern, high-performance learning platform.
