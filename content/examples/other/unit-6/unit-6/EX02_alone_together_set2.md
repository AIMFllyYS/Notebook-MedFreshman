## 阅读原文

> Turkle is not anti-technology. She acknowledges that digital tools have made life more convenient, more efficient, and more accessible in countless ways. Her concern is not with technology itself but with the way we let it mediate our most important relationships. The solution, she argues, is not to abandon our devices but to develop more mindful habits — creating tech-free zones in our homes, setting aside dedicated time for face-to-face conversation, and teaching children that some moments are too important to interrupt with a notification.
>
> Ultimately, Turkle's message is a call for intentionality. Technology is a powerful tool, but it is a tool that should serve human needs, not the other way around. By choosing when to connect and when to disconnect, when to text and when to talk, we can reclaim the genuine connections that make life meaningful. The goal is not to choose between the digital and the physical, but to integrate them in a way that honors our deepest need: to be truly present with one another.
>
> Critics of Turkle's work argue that she overstates the negative effects of technology. They point to studies showing that online communication can strengthen existing relationships, particularly for people separated by distance. Video calls allow grandparents to see their grandchildren, social media helps friends stay in touch across continents, and messaging apps enable collaboration that would have been impossible a generation ago.

---

**1.** According to Paragraph 1, Turkle's concern is with

A. technology itself being harmful

B. the way technology mediates important relationships

C. the cost of digital devices

D. the speed of technological change

**Answer: B**

**解析**（细节理解题）：第一段明确指出"Her concern is not with technology itself but with the way we let it mediate our most important relationships"。A项与"not with technology itself"矛盾；C项和D项在文中未提及。B项与原文完全一致。

---

**2.** Which of the following is NOT one of Turkle's suggested solutions?

A. Creating tech-free zones in homes

B. Setting aside time for face-to-face conversation

C. Abandoning all digital devices permanently

D. Teaching children that some moments are too important to interrupt

**Answer: C**

**解析**（细节排除题）：第一段列举了Turkle的三个建议：creating tech-free zones、setting aside dedicated time for face-to-face conversation、teaching children。同时明确指出"not to abandon our devices"。A、B、D项对应三个建议；C项"abandoning all digital devices permanently"与原文"not to abandon"直接矛盾。

---

**3.** The phrase "not the other way around" in Paragraph 2 means that

A. technology should serve human needs, not control them

B. humans should adapt to technology's requirements

C. technology and human needs are equally important

D. technology determines what humans need

**Answer: A**

**解析**（短语理解题）：*not the other way around* 意为"而非反过来"，前文是"technology should serve human needs"，因此"反过来"就是"人类服务于技术的需求"。A项准确解释了这一含义。B项和D项都是"the other way around"的含义，与原文相反；C项偏离了原文的对比结构。

---

**4.** What do critics of Turkle argue in Paragraph 3?

A. Technology has no effect on relationships at all.

B. Online communication can strengthen relationships, especially across distances.

C. Turkle's research methods are fundamentally flawed.

D. Face-to-face interaction is no longer necessary.

**Answer: B**

**解析**（细节理解题）：第三段指出批评者认为"she overstates the negative effects of technology"且"online communication can strengthen existing relationships, particularly for people separated by distance"。A项"no effect"过于极端；C项"research methods flawed"在文中未提及；D项"no longer necessary"与批评者的观点矛盾。B项与原文完全一致。

---

**5.** What is the main idea of this passage?

A. Technology should be abandoned to restore genuine human connection.

B. Technology is neither good nor bad; what matters is how intentionally we use it.

C. Online communication is always superior to face-to-face interaction.

D. Critics have proven that Turkle's concerns are baseless.

**Answer: B**

**解析**（主旨大意题）：文章呈现了Turkle的平衡观点（技术有用但需有意识使用）和批评者的反驳（在线交流也有益处），整体主旨是技术的价值取决于使用方式。A项与"not anti-technology"矛盾；C项"always superior"过于极端；D项"baseless"过于绝对，作者并未否定Turkle。B项 *neither good nor bad; what matters is how intentionally we use it* 准确概括了平衡的主旨。
