:::example{label=EX03 · The Business of Living — Set 1}

**阅读原文**（关键段落摘录）

> **Para 1:** According to a recent report, US entrepreneurship has reached a 14-year high. The report states that instead of necessity-driven new businesses, more US entrepreneurs are launching ventures based on perceived opportunities, growth ambitions and a broad optimistic outlook — a group sometimes called opportunity entrepreneurs. Nearly 78 percent of entrepreneurs last year started ventures to pursue new opportunities. That is up from 71 percent of total entrepreneurship in 2010.
>
> **Para 2:** There is an entrepreneur that exists deep down inside of each of us. Many people have thought about starting a business or have an idea for a new product, service or brand. People are extremely curious and they want to test the elements that drive their innate curiosity. Entrepreneurship is no longer just a business term — it's a way of life. You don't need to be an entrepreneur to be entrepreneurial. You just need to cultivate an entrepreneurial attitude.
>
> **Para 3:** John Cage famously said, "Our highest business is our daily life." Entrepreneurship shapes every aspect of my thoughts, my attitudes, my relationships and my general approach to working and living. Mine is a holistic entrepreneurship — one that applies to everyday life, on and off the job. Its effectiveness is not measured solely by revenue, profitability and / or social media followers, but also by influence, compassion and social impact. Its time is not bound by nine-to-five but by birth and death.
>
> **Para 4:** Through a broader view, entrepreneurship is not merely a capitalistic expression with a foundation in start-ups. It is also an existential expression with a foundation in pioneering life experience. When you learn to see daily difficulties, opportunities and responsibilities through the wider entrepreneurial lens, you optimize your whole life, because grand enterprises and gratifying lives spring from the same source. In short, the principles that drive business innovation, success and sustainability are the same ones that propel an exciting, revolutionary life.

---

**Question 1.** What is the main argument of this passage?

A. The rise in US entrepreneurship is driven primarily by financial necessity and economic hardship.

B. Entrepreneurship is a mindset and way of life that goes far beyond starting a business.

C. Measuring entrepreneurial success through revenue and profit margins is the most reliable approach.

D. Only people with a background in business or finance can truly develop an entrepreneurial spirit.

**Answer: B**

**解析**：主旨大意题。文章核心主张见 Para 2："Entrepreneurship is no longer just a business term — it's a way of life"，以及 Para 4 将创业精神定义为"an existential expression with a foundation in pioneering life experience"——创业精神本质是一种思维方式和生活哲学。

- **A 错误**：Para 1 明确指出趋势恰恰相反——当前是机会型创业（opportunity entrepreneurs）增多，而非被迫型。
- **B 正确**：与全文核心论点直接对应，创业精神是超越商业的生活方式和人生态度。
- **C 错误**：Para 3 明确指出有效性"not measured solely by revenue, profitability"，与此选项相悖。
- **D 错误**：Para 2 强调"You don't need to be an entrepreneur to be entrepreneurial"，明确否定了这一限定。

---

**Question 2.** According to Para 1, what distinguishes "opportunity entrepreneurs" from other entrepreneurs?

A. They have more access to venture capital and financial resources than other entrepreneurs.

B. They start businesses in response to market gaps identified through formal research.

C. They launch ventures driven by perceived opportunities and growth ambitions rather than necessity.

D. They are more likely to succeed because they operate in high-growth industries.

**Answer: C**

**解析**：细节理解题。Para 1 原文："instead of necessity-driven new businesses, more US entrepreneurs are launching ventures based on perceived opportunities, growth ambitions and a broad optimistic outlook — a group sometimes called opportunity entrepreneurs."

- **A 错误**：原文没有提到资金获取的差异。
- **B 错误**：原文没有提到"formal research"（正式研究），机会型创业者的动力是感知到的机遇，不必然来自系统研究。
- **C 正确**：与原文"perceived opportunities, growth ambitions"直接对应，相对于"necessity-driven"（被迫型）。
- **D 错误**：原文没有讨论不同类型企业家的成功率差异。

---

**Question 3.** The word "holistic" in Para 3 ("Mine is a holistic entrepreneurship") most probably means ______.

A. focused exclusively on financial and commercial outcomes

B. considering all aspects of life and work as an integrated whole

C. based on traditional and well-established business methods

D. limited to social enterprises and charitable organizations

**Answer: B**

**解析**：词义猜测题。"holistic"（整体性的）在语境中指"不局限于商业领域，适用于生活的各个方面，线上线下皆然"——即把工作和生活视为一个整体来对待。

- **A 错误**：Para 3 明确说明有效性"not measured solely by revenue, profitability"，与"exclusively financial"相反。
- **B 正确**："considering all aspects of life as an integrated whole"与 holistic（整体性的）的词义以及语境（"applies to everyday life, on and off the job"）完全吻合。
- **C 错误**："traditional and well-established"（传统而成熟）与 holistic 的语义无关。
- **D 错误**：Para 4 明确指出创业精神不仅仅是慈善或资本主义的表达，holistic 不限于社会企业。

**Question 4.** What can be inferred from the phrase "Its time is not bound by nine-to-five but by birth and death" in Para 3?

A. The author believes that entrepreneurs should work continuously without rest or personal time.

B. The author suggests that entrepreneurial thinking permeates one's entire life, not just working hours.

C. The author criticizes conventional employment for restricting workers to fixed hours.

D. The author implies that entrepreneurship is incompatible with a stable, long-term career.

**Answer: B**

**解析**：推理判断题。"nine-to-five"（朝九晚五）代表固定的工作时间框架；"birth and death"则暗示整个人生跨度。两者的对比表明：创业精神不受工作时间限制，而是贯穿人的整个生命。

- **A 错误**：原文是说创业精神的"时间跨度"，不是字面意义上鼓励不休息地工作。
- **B 正确**：准确推断出该比喻的含义——创业思维渗透整个人生，而非局限于工作时段。
- **C 错误**：原文没有对"传统就业模式"作批评性评价，只是用对比手法凸显创业精神的广度。
- **D 错误**：原文没有说创业精神与稳定长期职业"不相容"，二者并不矛盾。

**Question 5.** Which of the following best describes the author's overall tone in this passage?

A. Objective and detached, presenting data without personal investment in the subject.

B. Critical and cautionary, warning readers about the risks of the entrepreneurial lifestyle.

C. Inspirational and expansive, urging readers to adopt an entrepreneurial mindset in all areas of life.

D. Nostalgic and conservative, emphasizing the value of traditional business models.

**Answer: C**

**解析**：作者态度题。文章充满感召性语言——"You just need to cultivate an entrepreneurial attitude"、"you optimize your whole life"、"an exciting, revolutionary life"——作者积极鼓励读者将创业思维扩展到生活的各个方面，语气热情且富有感召力。

- **A 错误**：作者明显有个人立场（"Mine is a holistic entrepreneurship"），并非中立客观。
- **B 错误**：文章没有警告风险，而是强调创业精神的积极价值。
- **C 正确**：文章语气热情、鼓励性强，旨在拓展读者对"创业精神"的理解并激励行动。
- **D 错误**：作者强调的是全面而现代的创业精神，而非传统商业模式。
