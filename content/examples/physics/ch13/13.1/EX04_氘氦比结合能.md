:::example{label=氘氦比结合能}
**题目**：试计算氘核和氦原子核的结合能和比结合能。

**解**：
(1) 氘核 A=2, Z=1, $m_{D}=2.014\ 102u$

$$\begin{array}{l}\Delta E =\Delta m c ^ {2} =\left[ m _ {\mathrm{p}} + (A - Z) m _ {\mathrm{n}} - m _ {\mathrm{D}}\right] c ^ {2} = 931. 5\Delta m\\ = 931. 5\times (1. 00782503 + 1. 008665 - 2. 014102)\\ = 2. 22\mathrm{MeV}\\\varepsilon =\frac {\Delta E}{A} =\frac {2 . 22}{2} = 1. 11 (\mathrm{MeV})\\\end{array}
$$

(2) 氦核 A=4, Z=2, $m_{He}=4.002603u$

$$\begin{array}{l}\Delta E =\Delta m c ^ {2} =\left[ Z m _ {\mathrm{p}} + (A - Z) m _ {\mathrm{n}} - m _ {\mathrm{He}}\right] c ^ {2} = 931. 5\Delta m\\ = 931. 5\times (2\times 1. 00782503 + 2\times 1. 008665 - 4. 002603)\\ = 28. 30 (\mathrm{MeV})\\\varepsilon =\frac {\Delta E}{A} =\frac {28 . 30}{4} = 7. 07 (\mathrm{MeV})\\\end{array}
$$

**答案**：(2) 氦核 A=4, Z=2, $m_{He}=4.002603u$
